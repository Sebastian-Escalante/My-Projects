Rules

Welcome to Aggieland Defender

Reveille "The First Lady of Texas A&M" is in need of your help. In this game you will take control of your very own Reveille and help her defend the campus against those who wish to do it harm.

- Move Reveille by using the ARROW KEYS and attack using the SPACEBAR

- BEWARE of touching enemies or you will receive damage

- Everytime you defeat an enemy you will recieve 1 point

- Once all enemies in a WAVE are deafeted your health will regenerate along with reviving all dead players

- If all players die the game will END and the player with the highest score will be the WINNER
